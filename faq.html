Sign Up FAQ
  • Who are the assistants?
    They are responsible, friendly, and intuitive. They are carefully screened with background checks, references, and in-person interviews and comprehensively trained. They’ll meet you in person before your first visit, for a kick off meeting that makes sure you get everything you need.
  • What exactly will weekl.ie do?
    We can do any and all of the below; dropping off/picking up laundry & dry cleaning, grocery shopping, coordinating clothing repairs , getting fresh flowers, shipping packages, and much more! We can handle urgent requests too- think planning a kids party or getting that last minute Wedding gift.
  • So how many people are there behind weekl.ie?
    There’s a small team making sure we get everything done for you! Normally 3-5 people, made up of a Weeklie Lead who will be you key point of contact.
  • How do I get in touch with my weekl.ie team?
    An easy to use WebApp, SMS and Email service.
  • When do weekl.ie visit?
    We visit on the same day every week, normally weekdays between the hours of 9AM-6PM.
  • Can we do your shopping?
    Yep! We carefully select shops in your area: Grocery, Deli’s, Wine Shops, Dry Cleaners—you name it. We try to shop at the local places you like.
  • Does weekl.ie need my key?
    Yes, we need your key so all your tasks get done while you’re getting on with your life. We use a key exchange service that includes safe key storage, online key tracking and 24/7 support.
  • Where is weekl.ie available?
    Dublin.
